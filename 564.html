Syllabus

This course is meant as an introduction to point set and algebraic topology. Topics to be covered include but are not limited to:
Topological spaces and continuity
Compactness and connectedness
Metrizable topological spaces
Product topology and Tychonoff's theorem
The fundamental group and van Kampen's theorem
Covering spaces and the universal cover

The main text is Munkres, James R. Topology. 2nd ed., Prentice Hall, 2000.
